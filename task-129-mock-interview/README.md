# Task 129 — Mock interview simulation

**Category:** Cert & interview prep

## Goal
A simulated Sr. DBE interview covering design/tradeoff questions, not just syntax — the final task in this 130-task lab (0-129).

**Real-world scenario:** A real Sr. Snowflake DBE interview rarely asks
"write the CREATE STREAM syntax" — it asks "walk me through how you'd
design this pipeline" and listens for whether you've actually been
burned by the gotchas (late-arriving rows, masking-doesn't-block-WHERE,
UNDROP vs. Time Travel) or just memorized the syntax.

## Steps
1. Run `130_mock_interview.sql` — answer each question out loud before
   reading the "what a strong answer covers" notes.
2. For your weakest answer, go re-run the specific referenced task's
   SQL and re-answer from scratch.
3. Answer the understanding check below — there's no next task after
   this one.

## Understanding check
See the bottom of `130_mock_interview.sql` — three closing questions on
your weakest answer, why specificity matters more in a Sr. DBE
interview, and which three concepts from the whole lab matter most to
keep fresh.
